Converge Bio reference notes for the next redesign pass:

The strongest homepage pattern is a full-width hero that still preserves a disciplined content frame. The page uses a wide, cinematic first fold with a left-right split: large bold headline on the left, supporting paragraph and CTA on the right, then oversized visual product or graphic panels flowing directly underneath without cramped card boundaries.

The design language is simpler than the current Sapiens Health version. It relies on large dark background fields, very clear type hierarchy, generous spacing, and a few carefully placed graphic objects rather than many dense UI cards. The visual weight comes from oversized modules, soft 3D or rendered object imagery, and large rounded frames that feel like product showcases.

For Sapiens Health, the equivalent adaptation should be a full-screen healthcare AI landing page with broader width usage, larger graphic panels, and fewer small nested boxes. The hero should feel expansive on desktop, with the Sapiens Health logo and navigation above, a large headline block, short explanatory copy, a primary CTA group, and one or more large visual panels featuring medical imaging, clinical data, and framework visuals.

The framework section should no longer look like a research schematic. Instead it should behave like a clean product showcase: one panel for inputs with real medical imagery such as CT, pathology, waveform, and note snippets; one central branded MEDMIX engine treatment; and one output panel showing operational and clinical trial decision-support tasks. Graphics should include real or generated medical-looking visuals, icons, soft motion, and restrained animations.

The overall mood should stay dark and premium, but with Sapiens Health colors instead of copying the reference literally. Keep typography sharp and high-contrast, use fewer internal borders, and let each section breathe across the desktop width.
