# Nebius AI Discovery Award — Digital Health positioning notes

The Nebius AI Discovery Award page defines the **Digital Health** category as:

> "Transforming clinical workflows with AI-powered patient interaction and real-world data intelligence."

For Sapiens Health, the most relevant fit signals are the emphasis on **clinical workflows**, **real-world data intelligence**, and the broader qualification criteria around **AI-powered products**, **technological innovation**, **functionality and advantages**, **performance and efficiency**, **global impact**, and **market potential**.

This suggests the website should frame Sapiens Health as a **Digital Health AI company** rather than a broader multimodal platform spanning other verticals. The homepage should emphasize that MedMIX works on **incomplete multimodal clinical data**, turns fragmented hospital data into **actionable clinical insights**, and improves how care teams act under real-world data constraints.

The site should avoid biomanufacturing language for this version because that dilutes category fit. Instead, it should foreground hospital and care-delivery use cases, prediction tasks that become workflow insights, and a concise articulation of why incomplete data is the operational reality in healthcare.
