## About pipeline revision review

The About section now reads much more like a business-facing product pipeline than a research diagram. The input side shows a broader and more realistic span of hospital and study data, the MEDMIX engine is visibly branded at the center, and the output side now surfaces understandable prediction tasks such as OR time estimation, discharge readiness, ICU escalation, trial eligibility, and patient matching.

The preview confirms that the layout feels more dynamic because the input cards, engine, and output cards now carry animated motion. The structure also makes the user journey clearer: data enters, the engine processes incomplete modalities, and a decision-support workspace emerges on the output side.
