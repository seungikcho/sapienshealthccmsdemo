# Diffuse-inspired direction for Sapiens Health

The requested direction is a dark, minimal, deep-tech homepage with very high headline contrast, a narrow centered hero composition, sparse top navigation, and a single announcement pill above the hero. The overall feel should be more product-launch and frontier-technology than editorial or academic.

For Sapiens Health, the adapted structure should use only three navigation items: About, Research, and Contact. The top pill should read "Launching MEDMIX I: Best multimodal AI engine in digital health". The hero should use the Sapiens Health logo, a compact centered badge treatment, and a large two-line headline reading "Intelligence layer for incomplete clinical data." The subcopy should read "Sapiens Health builds the AI engine that fuses imaging, EHR, and clinical data — even when some are missing."

The page should keep the healthcare AI deep-tech atmosphere while avoiding direct duplication. The design should rely on a dark background, luminous but restrained line work, subtle purple-blue healthcare-toned glow, sharp typography, simple borders, and two clear call-to-action buttons: About Technology and Get In Touch.
